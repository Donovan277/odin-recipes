Chocolate Mug Cake

What is it?

A mug cake like the name suggests is a cake that is most often made in a cup or mug then cooked in a microwave. These mini cakes can also be made using a ramekin and oven, but the idea is a quick and easy one serving treat that can be made in 5-10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour ($0.02)
  • 1 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder ($0.03)
  • 2 tsp sugar ($0.01)
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder ($0.02)
  • a pinch salt ($0.01)
  • 1 Tbsp cooking oil ($0.04)
  • 2 Tbsp milk ($0.04)
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter ($0.13)
